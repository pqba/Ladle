Ladle's Purpose

Designed for curious minds

Ladle is designed from the ground up to be minimalist yet highly useful.

By choosing a subreddit specific list, seeing the title and rating of content and not engaging with the endless scrolling you can take the benefits of open discussion, varied communities, and insightful topics without the overload and distraction of the main site.

This project is open source, and you can create own virtual bot which facilitates this content for you, taking complete control of your data and feed.
